Our Story

Grab a seat by the camp fire and join us as we tell the story behind Ready Set Travel.

From the moment we first stepped foot outside of the countries we call home into other nations around the world, we knew in our hearts that travel was our biggest passion. Out of this passion, Ready Set Travel was born. That is why here at Ready Set Travel, we pride ourselves on creating new and exciting experiences and adventures for our fellow travelers. Each and every experience we create is meticulously planned and tested out by our own team members before it is released to our clients. Our experiences are not only top of the the line but they are also very affordable because we believe everyone should be able to experience all that the world has to offer without it costing an arm and a leg. By putting people first and carefully planning each trip, we offer our clients top of the line travel experiences around the world. So when you’re ready to schedule your next trip, we will be there ready to put together a once in a lifetime vacation for you and whoever you want to bring along with you. Get ready, get set, and let’s travel!
